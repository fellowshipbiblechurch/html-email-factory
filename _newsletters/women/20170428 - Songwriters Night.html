Fellowship Women,

I’ll never forget when I first moved to Nashville, Amy Grant held the door open for my daughter and I as we exited a dance class in Green Hills. We’ve chatted with Brandon Heath at Jeni’s Splendid Ice Creams and worked with several famous songwriters on middle school events at my daughter’s school.

This is Music City after all and we are surrounded by talent!

Fellowship Women would like to celebrate the gifted female singer/songwriters right here in our church body. We hope you’ll come enjoy this songwriters experience and take time for a night out with friends.

  • Friday, May 5th
  • The Back Barn
  • 7:00pm

“Sing to Him a new song; Play skillfully with a shout of joy.” Psalm 33:3
