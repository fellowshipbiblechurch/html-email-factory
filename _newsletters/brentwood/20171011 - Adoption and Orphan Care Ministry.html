Tennessee Alliance for Kids

Did you know that Fellowship helped launch a non-profit? Tennessee Alliance for Kids (TAK) serves as a liaison between the faith-based community and the State of Tennessee with the goal of meeting the immediate needs of children and youth currently in or aging out of foster care. Since its inception, just over a year ago, TAK has received over 75 requests representing 190 kids, kept 27 children in their homes and saved the state of TN over $727,000! Through the #FOSTERLOVE Coalition, needs of children at risk of coming into foster care, those in care, or those aging out of care are shared with individuals, churches, and businesses through email and social media. TAK then works to ensure that donors are matched to meet the needs. To learn more about TAK, visit tnallianceforkids.org. Making a difference for a child in foster care is easy… simply like us on Facebook at TNAllianceForKids today!

Re | Engage Marriage Ministry

Re | Engage is a marriage enrichment program that examines God’s design for marriage and applies biblical principles to guide couples towards growth in their relationship. Re | Engage offers hope to marriages by helping couples move toward oneness through stories of grace, teaching and small groups. Beginning in January, this 16-week experience will meet at Fellowship (Brentwood campus). If interested in this joining this class with other adoptive/fostering couples, please email Connie Dunlop (cdunlop@fbctn.org).
