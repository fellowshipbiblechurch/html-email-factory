Nashville Couples,

We’re so excited about our new marriage class, A Promise Kept, starting next week and wanted to let you know we have six spots available (for six couples). If you’re looking to spend some time giving your marriage a dose of encouragement, then this class is for you.

In this five-week class we’ll talk about the pain of marital drift, the power of a lifestyle of confession and forgiveness and the pathway of renewed intimacy. Class starts next Thursday, October 5th, 6:30pm, The Barn (Brentwood campus). Please consider joining us by registering below. Hope to see you there!
