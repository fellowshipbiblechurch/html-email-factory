Dear Fellowship Kids Volunteer,

We are excited about your interest in Fellowship Kids and are looking forward to partnering with you. By serving our children, you are in for the adventure of a lifetime and we pray you will be blessed beyond measure.

Before we can assign you a permanent placement in Fellowship Kids, we ask all volunteers—new and returning—to complete the Fellowship Kids Volunteer Application (link below) within two days. Once your forms have been submitted and accepted, you will be contacted and your placement confirmed. In the meantime, please mark your calendar for one of our mandatory Volunteer Training Events. It's a great time to meet other volunteers and to get inspired and equipped for the year ahead.

Attendance at one of these training events is required before serving with Fellowship Kids:

  • Tuesday, Aug. 8th (6:30-8:30pm in the Main Lobby)
  • Thursday, Aug. 10th (11:30am-1:30pm in the Main Lobby)
  • Saturday, Aug. 12th (9:00-11:00am in the Worship Center; breakfast and check-in begins at 8:30am)

If you have any questions, please let us know. Thanks for taking the first steps in serving with Fellowship Kids.
