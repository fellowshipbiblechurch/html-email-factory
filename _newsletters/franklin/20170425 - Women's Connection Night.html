Fellowship Women,

You’re invited to our Women’s Connection Night this Tuesday, April 25th, 6:30-8:30pm, at Fellowship Franklin. This evening is about gathering, sharing, and growing with women in different season of life. It’s a time to to get to know others and be known. We'll have several women sharing throughout the night, along with honest conversations around tables together. No RSVP required. Yummy desserts will be served.

Hope you'll join us!
