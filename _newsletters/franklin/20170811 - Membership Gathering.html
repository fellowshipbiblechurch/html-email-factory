Fellowship Franklin Family,

You’re invited to join us for a Membership Gathering on Sunday, Aug. 27th from 5:45-7:30pm. We will enjoy time connecting with others in our church body, and casting vision for our role as members. We will enjoy desserts together—so we ask every family to bring one sweet treat to share. Please let us know if you plan to attend below.

There is limited childcare for children birth – 4th grade, so sign your family up this week to make sure you have a spot!
