Fellowship Dads,

Registration closes tomorrow, Friday, Feb. 24th for Fellowship’s Daddy Daughter Dance on March 3rd. You won’t want to miss out on this fun evening designed just for you and your daughters! There will be dancing, desserts, a chocolate fountain, and an optional photo session. This is a great opportunity to create some special memories that she will treasure for years to come. Hope to see you there!

  • Friday, March 3rd, 6:30-9:30pm
  • Brentwood Campus, Worship Center
  • Cost: $50/1 daughter; $60/2 daughters; $70/3+ daughters
