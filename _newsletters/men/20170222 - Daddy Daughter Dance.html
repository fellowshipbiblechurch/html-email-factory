Fellowship Dads,


Registration closes next Wednesday, March 1st for Fellowship’s Daddy Daughter Dance on Friday, March 3rd at the Brentwood campus. You won’t want to miss out on this fun evening designed just for you and your daughters! There will be dancing, desserts, a chocolate fountain, and an optional photo session.

Pricing is simple: $50 for you and one daughter, then $10 more per daughter up to $70 total. Also, don't forget to sign up for a photo slot! This is a great opportunity to create some special memories that she will treasure for years to come. Hope to see you there!
