Fellowship Men,

We are kicking off our Men's Ministry next Thursday evening, September 7th at 6:30pm with our next MAN TO MAN event (Worship Center, Brentwood Campus). Trust me, you won't want to miss this!

At this event you will also learn about men's groups, classes, and outreach opportunities that you can jump into this fall. Snacks will be served. Please register so that we can plan for room setup and food!
