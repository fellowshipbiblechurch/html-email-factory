Fellowship Men,

If you weren't able to attend our Man To Man kickoff event last night, below is a list of groups and classes that are launching this month. I’m excited to see what God will do in and through us as we interact with His Word and each other to mature in the faith and give our lives away. Here some of our upcoming studies:

Men's 27:17 Groups

Proverbs 27:17 says "As iron sharpens iron, so one man sharpens another." If you're interested in connecting with other men around the Word of God for a year, these groups meet off campus every other week. Registration is open until Wed., September 20th. Groups will be forming the last week in September.

Authentic Manhood

Authentic Manhood is our six-week class that meets at two different times, Thursday evenings and Friday mornings. This semester we’ll focus on A Man and His Design.

Inside Looking Out

What if my life was an illustration of what Jesus taught the disciples and apostles? It can be! Join other Fellowship men on Friday mornings as we challenge each other to be disciples.

Growing in Christ

Join other men on Saturday mornings for a study through the Book of Galatians. We’ll discover how the gospel is not only the means by which we come to faith, but also the way that we grow in our faith!

Also, mark your Calendar for the next two Man To Man Events: Friday, October 6th, at 6:15am (guest speaker Nate Larkin) and Thursday, November 16th, at 6:30pm. Details to follow.
